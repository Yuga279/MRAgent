## About us

Acme Gadgets sells smart-home devices online. Support hours are Monday to
Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time. Customers can also email
support@acmegadgets.example.

## Shipping

- Standard shipping: 3–5 business days, free on orders over $50, otherwise $4.99.
- Express shipping: 1–2 business days, $12.99.
- We ship to the United States and Canada only.
- Orders placed before 2 PM Eastern ship the same day.

## Returns and refunds

- 30-day return window from the delivery date.
- Items must be unused and in original packaging.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days
  after we receive the return.
- To start a return, customers give us their order number and we email them a
  prepaid return label.

## Warranty

- All devices carry a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
- Water damage and accidental drops are not covered.
